# Arbiter

Your AI agent that negotiates crypto trades across multiple venues.

## What it does

- **Compare prices** across OKX Smart Trading, Binance, and 1inch simultaneously
- **Negotiate** with OKX's Smart Trading AI to get better OTC prices
- **Execute trades** on the best venue with full transparency

## Usage

Ask Arbiter to find you the best price:

- "Compare prices for 1 ETH"
- "Buy 0.5 BTC at the best price"
- "Negotiate with OKX for 10 SOL"

## Tools

- `compare_prices` — Query all venues and rank by total cost
- `negotiate_okx` — Haggle with OKX Smart Trading for a better price
- `execute_trade` — Execute on the best venue
- `get_supported_tokens` — List supported tokens and prices

Currently running in demo mode with simulated prices. Connect your API keys for live trading.
